# Introduction
Markdown is a text-based language invented by John Gruber at 2004. Thanks to its composition of simple texts and symbols, writing and reading of Markdown-based text is quite straightforward.

# Headers
    # H1
    ## H2
    ### H3
    #### H4
    ##### H5
    ###### H6

*Note: Higher number states inner headings.*

# Blockquote
    > First blockquote
    >   > Second blockquote
    >   >   > Third blockquote

# List
### Listed by numbers
    1. First
    2. Second
    3. Third
### Listed by bullet points
    * apple
        * banana
            * coconut
    + apple
        + banana
            + coconut
    - apple
        - banana
            - coconut

All goes same bullet points.

    * apple
        + banana
            - coconut
                + donkey

Mixed bullet points are possible.

### Horizontal line
```
* * *
***
*****
- - -
--------------------------------
```
All above makes a horizontal line.
